Demand-based appreciation

Pricing engine that rewards real marketplace demand.

This prototype updates each asset's estimated share price from buyer demand, filled trades, profile views, watchlists, ownership utilization, and available supply. It is a demo algorithm, not financial advice or a production securities exchange.

price = base × (1 + demand + utilization + momentum + scarcity)
Demand signals
  • Buy orders and completed trades
  • Views and watchlist saves
  • Share utilization and remaining supply
  • Recent price momentum
